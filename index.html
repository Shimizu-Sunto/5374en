Attentions

●Please do not leave any garbage other than burnable garbage, unburnable garbage and plastic garbage at your usual collection place.

●Whenever you need to throw away used batteries, please bring to the town 　hall or your district community center where collection cans are set.


●We do not collect any industrial refuse.


●We do not collect any dangerous substance such as tire, motorbike, battery, used oil, and chemical as in agricultural chemicals.


●TV, refrigerator, washing machine, air conditioner, drying machine and computer would not be collected.



For the separation of garbage etc., please visit the website of Shimizu Town or contact the Shimizu Town Office Regional Promotion Division.

Large-size refuse
Large-size refuse needs reservation. Please contact the large-size refuse collector and individually take the productsto the place.(Phone number for reservation 055-971-0530)

①Contact the large-size refuse collector for reservation.
●Call the large-size refuse collector or drop by the place to check available dates to bring in your refuse.
●9：00～16：00　（Reservation/Delivery time）
●Contact and give details such as: your address, name, telephone number, large-size refuse you wish to bring and number of items.(We would not accept a reservation on the same day of delivery or additional item(s) that you have previously declared before the day.）
②Deliver your refuse to the large-size refuse collection place.
●The collection place could be crowded. Be careful when you drive your car in.
③Matters that require attention when delivering.
●Papers, old clothes and cardboard need to be cross-tied with white paper cord.
●Please follow the instructions of a staff and unload the item by yourself.
●You can only bring item(s) full of one pickup truck per delivery.
●There are things that we can not accept. Please check the details when you reserve.
●We will need to identify that you are a town resident before we accept your refuse. Please bring your driver’s license or other documents as a bill for public service charge, which can prove that you are a resident of thetown.

    About Other's garbage

    Dry batteries:

  • There is Recycling cans etc. in office, town community exchange center, town disaster prevention center, public hall of each district, electric store.
    You can put them out at any time. Please do not mix it with other garbage.

    • Cloth:

  • Please use collective collection of each district and service of resource processing company.

    • Milk carton:

  • There is a collection box at the office, town community exchange center. Please wash the inside, cut it open, please dry and let out.

    • Garbage dumped from business establishments (general waste)

    Garbage taken from business establishments (corporations / individuals) is not collected in the town.
    Please do not put it out to the garbage station of the district.
    Please contract with authorized dealer at each business office and discharge.
    We can’t contract with non-permitter of town.
    For industrial waste, please see Shizuoka prefecture homepage.

    About 5374.jp
    The problem of garbage is getting serious in every area. In Code for Kanazawa, first of all we looked at how to dispose of the correct garbage. The purpose and usage are very simple.
    ※This application corresponds to '5374.jp' developed by Code for Kanazawa, Shimizu-cho version of Shizuoka prefecture.

    Purpose

    "When, which garbage is collected?"
    For example, when you moved to Shimizu-cho in Sunto-gun or when you live there, it is designed so that you can understand immediately using this application.

    How to use

    Show garbage genre by color

    The day of the garbage nearest from today and its genre are displayed in order from the top.

    Garbage that can be thrown away

    When you tap the garbage genre, you can see a list of garbage that you can throw away.

    Settings

    When you select your area, the garbage collection date is automatically updated.

    About the information of garbage provided

    We analyzed and implemented it in the application from garbage collection calendar in Shimizu-cho, Sunto-gun
